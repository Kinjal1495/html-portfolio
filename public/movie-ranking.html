The Best Movies According to Kinjal

My top 3 movies of all-time

Spirited Away

Directed by Hayao Miyazaki, Spirited Away is a Japanese animated film that follows the story of a young girl.

Frozen

Directed by Chris Buck and Jennifer Lee, Frozen is a Disney animated film that tells the story of two sisters.

Moana

Directed by Ron Clements and John Musker, Moana is a Disney animated film that follows the story of a young adventurer.
